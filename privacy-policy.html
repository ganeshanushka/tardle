What information we collect

Depending on how you use the game, we may collect:

• Email address (only if you choose to sign up)

• Gameplay data (such as completed puzzles, guesses, or streaks)

• Basic technical information (such as browser type) needed for the game to function properly

You can play the game without creating an account.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

• Save your game progress and preferences

• Send optional reminder or update emails

• Maintain, improve, and secure the game

Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Your data may be processed by trusted service providers (such as Google Firebase) solely to operate and maintain the game.

Data storage & security

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is completely secure.

Children's privacy

This game is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
