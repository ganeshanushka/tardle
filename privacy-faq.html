Do I have to create an account to play?

No. You can play the game without signing up. Some features (like saved progress or streaks) may require an account.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Do you track me across other websites?

No. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising technologies.

Why do you collect email addresses?

Email addresses are collected only if you choose to provide one, and are used to:

• Save your progress

• Send optional reminder or update emails

Who can see my data?

Your data is only accessible to the game's systems and service providers needed to operate the game (such as Firebase).
