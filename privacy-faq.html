Is an account required to play?

No, an account is not required to play Tardle. You can enjoy the game without creating an account. However, certain features such as saving your game progress, maintaining streaks, and accessing statistics require account registration.

Do you sell or share my personal information?

No. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data is used solely to provide and improve the game service.

Do you track my activity across other websites?

No. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies, advertising networks, or any technologies that monitor your activity on other websites. Our use of cookies and local storage is limited to essential game functionality.

Why do you collect email addresses?

Email addresses are collected only when you voluntarily create an account. We use email addresses for the following purposes:

• To authenticate your account and secure your game data

• To save your game progress and statistics

• To send optional daily reminder emails (if you subscribe)

• To send important service-related communications

You can unsubscribe from reminder emails at any time through your account settings.

Who has access to my data?

Your data is accessible only to our authorized systems and trusted service providers that are necessary to operate the game. These service providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it solely for the purpose of providing the service. We do not grant access to your personal information to advertisers, marketers, or other third parties.

How is my data stored and secured?

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. Your data is stored on secure servers managed by trusted service providers. While we take reasonable precautions to safeguard your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

How long do you keep my data?

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the service. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with our data retention policies, except where we are required to retain certain information for legal or operational purposes.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You can delete your account and associated data at any time through your account settings. Once deleted, your personal information will be removed from our active systems, though some information may remain in backup systems for a limited period as part of our standard data retention practices.

What rights do I have regarding my data?

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data

• Correct inaccurate information

• Request deletion of your account and data

• Opt out of optional email communications

• Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us at tardlehelp@gmail.com.

Do you share data with third parties?

We only share data with third-party service providers that are essential to operating the game (such as hosting and authentication services). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it solely for the purpose of providing services to us. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties for their own purposes.

What happens if there's a data breach?

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law. We take data security seriously and have measures in place to prevent, detect, and respond to security incidents.
