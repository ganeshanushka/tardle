Is an account required to play?

No. You can play without an account. Accounts are only needed to save your progress, track streaks, and view statistics.

Do you sell or share my personal information?

No. We don't sell or share your data with anyone.

Do you track my activity across other websites?

No. We only use cookies and local storage to make the game work. We don't track you on other sites.

Why do you collect email addresses?

We only collect your email if you create an account. We use it to:

• Save your game progress

• Send optional daily reminders (if you sign up)

• Send important account updates

You can unsubscribe from reminders anytime in your account settings.

Who has access to my data?

Only our systems and the service providers we use to run the game. We don't give access to advertisers or marketers.

How is my data stored and secured?

We use standard security practices like encryption. Your data is stored on secure servers. While we do our best to protect your information, no system is 100% secure.

How long do you keep my data?

We keep your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we'll remove your personal information, though some may stay in backups for a short time.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You can delete your account anytime in your account settings. Your data will be removed from our systems.

What rights do I have regarding my data?

You can:

• Access your data

• Correct mistakes

• Delete your account

• Unsubscribe from emails

Contact us at tardlehelp@gmail.com if you need help with any of these.

Do you share data with third parties?

Only with the service providers we need to run the game (like hosting). They're required to protect your data. We don't share with advertisers or data brokers.

What happens if there's a data breach?

If your data is affected, we'll notify you and the authorities as required by law. We take security seriously and have measures in place to prevent breaches.
