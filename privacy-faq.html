Do I need to create an account to play Tardle?

No, creating an account is completely optional. You can play Tardle without signing up. However, certain features such as saving your game progress, maintaining streaks, and accessing statistics require an account. Creating an account is free and allows you to enhance your gaming experience.

Do you sell or share my personal information?

No. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your data.

Do you track me across other websites?

No. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies, advertising technologies, or any methods to track your activity on other websites. We only collect information necessary for the game to function properly and to provide you with the services you request.

Why do you collect email addresses?

Email addresses are collected only if you voluntarily choose to create an account. We use email addresses for the following purposes:

• To create and manage your account

• To save your game progress and statistics

• To send you optional daily reminder emails (if you subscribe)

• To send important account-related notifications

You can unsubscribe from reminder emails at any time through your account settings or by using the unsubscribe link in the emails.

How is my data stored and secured?

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a trusted cloud service provider. Firebase provides enterprise-grade security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. We implement reasonable security practices to protect your information, though no system is completely secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your account.

Who has access to my data?

Your data is accessible only to:

• You (through your account)

• Our game systems necessary to operate Tardle

• Google Firebase, our trusted service provider that hosts and secures the data

We do not share your data with advertisers, marketers, or any other third parties. Firebase processes your data solely to provide hosting and authentication services for Tardle.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through your account settings. Once deleted, your account information, game statistics, and progress will be permanently removed from our systems. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

What cookies or tracking technologies do you use?

We use essential cookies and local storage technologies to:

• Keep you signed in to your account

• Save your game preferences and progress

• Ensure the game functions correctly

We do not use cookies for advertising, marketing, or cross-site tracking. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain game features.

Is Tardle appropriate for children?

Tardle is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at tardlehelp@gmail.com and we will delete such information.

Will you notify me if the privacy policy changes?

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the Privacy Policy page. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of Tardle after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
